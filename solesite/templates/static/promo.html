SOLE
Self organizing learning

SOLE is a site that brings students together. It's a fully customizable application that takes care of the signup, activation, messaging and more. It's GPL licensed, which means you can use it for free!

Got any feedback? For bugs you can report an issue at Github.

  • What is SOLE?

    SOLE is an django application that supplies your project with full account management. It handles the signup, activation, signin etc. for you. It also supplies your users with an optional profile model that integrates gravatar avatars. Signup at this website and try it out for yourself.

  • It's free!

    SOLE is licensed under the GPL license. This means it's free to use. You don't have to worry about any licensing..

  • Messaging

    SOLE comes with an optional, chat-like, messaging system. This allows your users to communicate with each other through your site. It's use is intuitive and easy!
